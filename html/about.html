Northern Secondary School

Located near the heart of Toronto at 851 Mt Pleasant Rd, is where this all started: Northern Secondary School, or as we like to call it NSS. Northern is a large school with almost eighteen hundred students and was founded at September 1930. There are many programs that NSS offers such as the SHSM Program, gifted, technology and business streams.

What We've Been Doing

Thanks to the generous support of our sponsors, parents, students, School Foundation, Parent Council, School Council, our school, and the tech department, we have been able to participate in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) this year. FRC is a competition where international high school teams design, build and program a large size robot to compete in a themed game. Throughout build-season we have been able to develop our skills in using computer aided design (CAD) software, manufacturing tools including computerized numerical control (CNC) machine, 3D printer, coding, electronics control system, business development and problem-solving.

Our Subteams

Our sub-teams are groups of students that focus on different aspects of the robot and team operations. They include our Business, Programming, Manufacturing, Design, and the Web Design and Media Teams.

Design Team

The team in charge of designing the robot using CAD. In our case we used Fusion 360 to design and model various parts and components for the robot.

Manufacturing Team

All the assembling and putting together the robot happens here. This team takes the designs created by the design team and fabricates them into a fully functioning robot.
